My Favorite animes according to Miles

My top three animes of all time.

Re Zero - Starting life in a New World

This is my most favorite anime of all time. It's an isekai that is exciting and you can't wait until the next episode.

Demon Slayer

All around good anime. Good animation. Good story. Very popular anime as of 2024. Totally recommended for people!

The Rising of the Shield Hero

This anime has a great story about a person who gets isekai'd into another world. He has to face everything that comes to him.
